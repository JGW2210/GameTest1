        How to play

        A random pathogenic bacterium is chosen. You're told its Kingdom (always Bacteria). Identify it in as few guesses as possible. Three modes:

        • Taxonomic — guess a genus + species; every matching rank (Phylum → Species) is confirmed and the dendrogram grows down the trunk. Wrong branches collapse into clickable pills where they leave the correct path — a fresh guess is shown in full for a moment, then folds away; click a pill to expand that branch again. A ? marks where the true lineage continues.
        • Identifier — a grid of lab results (Gram stain, catalase, haemolysis, fermentation…). Each guess colours cells green (exact), orange (partial/variable) or red (no match). Green cells lock; every other cell mirrors your last guess.
        • Combined — both surfaces update together.

        Taxonomy uses current (post-2021 ICNP) nomenclature — e.g. Firmicutes → Bacillota, Proteobacteria → Pseudomonadota. You can also load your own bacteria lists from Supabase (see src/config.js).
