        How to play

        A random pathogenic bacterium has been chosen. You're told its Kingdom (always Bacteria) — everything below is hidden.

        1. Guess a genus and species. Pick a genus from the suggestions.
        2. Every rank of your guess that matches the hidden answer — Phylum, Class, Order, Family, Genus, Species — is confirmed and revealed to you.
        3. The dendrogram grows down through the confirmed ranks. Where your guess diverges from the truth, it branches off, and a ? marks the direction the real answer took.
        4. Use the confirmed ranks to narrow in. Solve it in as few guesses as possible.

        Taxonomy is normalized to current (post-2021 ICNP) nomenclature — e.g. Firmicutes → Bacillota, Proteobacteria → Pseudomonadota.
